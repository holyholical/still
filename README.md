# still

**still** is a privacy-focused notes app designed to keep your thoughts local first, then sync to the cloud when you sign in. It offers a seamless, distraction-free environment to jot down your ideas instantly with automatic saving — no need to worry about losing your work.

---

## Features

- **Privacy-first**  
  Your notes are saved locally by default, ensuring your data stays private until you choose to sign in and sync with the cloud.

- **Instant typing and syncing**  
  Type instantly and have your notes sync seamlessly when you sign in.

- **Note sharing (coming soon)**  
  Share your notes with others and collaborate in real-time. *(Note: Collaboration is a planned feature and currently not functional.)*

- **Simple and clean interface**  
  Focus on your thoughts with a minimal, easy-to-use interface.

- **Automatic saving**  
  No need to manually save. We save your notes continuously so you never lose progress.

---

## How to Use

1. Start typing your notes locally.  
2. Sign in to sync your notes to the cloud for backup and access across devices.  
3. Create new notes easily using the `+ new note` button.  
4. Share notes and collaborate with others once the collaboration feature is enabled.

---

## Current Status

- The app is fully functional for local note-taking and syncing when signed in.
- Collaboration and note sharing are features in development and will be available soon.

---

## Privacy & Security

- Notes remain on your device unless you sign in to sync them.
- No data is shared without your explicit action.
- Collaboration features will be designed with end-user privacy as a priority.
